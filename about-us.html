About Us

Founded in 2024 in Dhaka, Bangladesh, Subterranean Engineering & Construction (E&C) is a sole proprietorship company specializing in deep foundation and underground infrastructure. Our focus is on contributing to major infrastructure projects across the country, shaping the future of Bangladesh’s urban and industrial landscape.

With a team of experts experienced both locally and internationally, we are committed to delivering safe, sustainable, and high-quality projects. Our dedication to excellence ensures that we consistently exceed stakeholder expectations while fostering trust, transparency, and professionalism in every aspect of our work.

At Subterranean E&C, we aim to become a reliable partner in engineering and construction, helping to build a sustainable future. By prioritizing quality, integrity, and client success, we uphold our promise to contribute to the progress of Bangladesh.
